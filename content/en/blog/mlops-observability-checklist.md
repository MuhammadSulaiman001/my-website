When an ML model reaches production, reliability matters as much as accuracy.  
This checklist helps keep AI APIs healthy over time:

- Monitor response latency and error rate per endpoint.
- Track data drift for high-impact features.
- Compare online predictions with delayed ground truth.
- Alert on sudden drops in model confidence.
- Maintain dashboards for model version and rollout status.

A strong observability setup shortens incident response and improves trust in AI systems.
