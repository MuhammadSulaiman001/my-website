AI delivery succeeds when technical and business teams move with shared priorities.

What works well in practice:

- Define measurable outcomes before model experimentation.
- Break delivery into short milestones with demoable value.
- Use explicit ownership across data, backend, and MLOps.
- Track risks early: data quality, latency budgets, compliance.
- Close each sprint with decisions, not only status updates.

Clear communication and operational discipline are key to sustainable AI execution.
